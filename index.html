Development Timeline

Week 1-2: Newborn Stage

0-2 weeks old

Eyes closed, completely dependent on mother. Sleeping 90% of the day. Starting to develop sense of smell and touch.

Week 3-4: Opening Eyes

3-4 weeks old

Eyes opening to reveal beautiful blue color. Beginning to take wobbly first steps. Starting to show curiosity about surroundings.

Week 5-8: Socialization

5-8 weeks old

Playful personality emerging! Learning to interact with siblings. Beginning to use litter box. Teeth coming in and starting to eat solid food.

Week 9-12: Growing Up

9-12 weeks old

Fully weaned and independent. Coordinated movements and agile climbing. Personality fully developed. Ready for forever home!

Month 4-6: Young Adult

4-6 months old

Rapid growth phase. Adult teeth replacing baby teeth. Full of energy and mischief. Learning boundaries and house rules.

Month 7-12: Adolescence

7-12 months old

Reaching adult size. Personality maturing. Strong bond formed with family. Playful but showing more independence.
